About SocPoliTech Insight

SocPoliTech Insight bridges social research, data science, and public policy — is a socio-political data and insight platform that bridges the worlds of social research, data science, and public policy. We help stakeholders — including academics, NGOs, media, political actors, company and public institutions — collect, analyze, and visualize complex data in a way that is both rigorous and accessible.

Data Focus

Our research and data analysis focus on exploring socio-political, economic, and digital transformations through evidence-based insights and tailored datasets.

Social Political & Governance Insight

Mapping social political dynamics, institutional interactions, and public policy trends through structured datasets and case-based monitoring.

Economic & Business Data

Tracking patterns from open economic data, enterprise networks, and market trends to understand regional and sectoral dynamics.

Media & Information

Analyzing framing, narratives, and sentiment across traditional and digital media ecosystems.

Technology & Innovation

Developing tools for web scraping, data visualization, and AI-based interpretation to enhance research transparency and efficiency.
